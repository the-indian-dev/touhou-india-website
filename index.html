Indian Touhou Community

We're a chill Hindi-speaking fan group from India, dedicated to the entire Touhou (東方) series! Our server is a welcoming space for all Indian fans, even if you don't speak Hindi. We dive into everything Gensokyo: discussing the lore, sharing epic fanworks, enjoying the series' iconic music, and tackling the challenging bullet hell (danmaku) games. Beyond Touhou, our interests are wide, and you'll find us playing other games like Osu!, Geometry Dash, and Undertale. Our server has minimal rules for a fun and relaxed experience—just don't flame others or post racist stuff. So, whether you're a seasoned player with 10 lunatic 1ccs, a fumo collector, or just here to share the best Allu Arjun memes, you'll fit right in!

We use a Discord server for the community; this website doesn't host anything itself except for the server invite. You will need a Discord account to join our server; their terms and privacy policy apply there.

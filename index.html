Indian Touhou Community
We're a chill Hindi-speaking Touhou fan group from India! Everyone is welcome here, even if you don't speak Hindi. Besides Touhou, we also enjoy playing other games like Osu!, Geometry Dash, Undertale, and much more. Our server has minimal rules for an enjoyable experience, with the general rule being: just don't flame other people in the server and don't post racist stuff. Whether you are a dude with 10 1cc lunatic runs, someone who just likes their fumos, or you just want to shitpost Allu Arjun memes, everyone is welcome to join this server!
We use a Discord server for the community; this website doesn't host anything itself except for the server invite. You will need a Discord account to join our server; their terms and privacy policy apply there.

Please note that this server is not associated with Team Shanghai Alice or ZUN; it's an unofficial fan-made Discord server!

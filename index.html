Indian Touhou Community

Discord Fanart Gallery r/TouhouIndia

Welcome to India's premier Touhou Project (東方) Discord community! We're a vibrant Hindi-speaking fan group celebrating ZUN's legendary bullet hell series. Our server welcomes all Indian Touhou enthusiasts, regardless of language preference.

Immerse yourself in the magical world of Gensokyo with fellow fans as we discuss intricate lore, share pretty fanart and doujin works, discuss ZUN's epic fire music, and master the challenging danmaku gameplay mechanics. From Reimu Hakurei's shrine maiden adventures to Marisa Kirisame's magical pursuits, we cover every aspect of the Touhou universe.

Our diverse gaming community extends beyond Touhou to include rhythm games like Osu!, platformers like Geometry Dash, and indie gems like Undertale. Whether you're a hardcore player with multiple Lunatic 1CC clears, a dedicated fumo plushie collector, a doujin music enthusiast, or simply love sharing quality memes, you'll find your place in our friendly, low-moderation environment designed for maximum fun and authentic fan interaction.

We use a Discord server for the community; this website doesn't host anything itself except for the server invite. You will need a Discord account to join our server; their terms and privacy policy apply there.
